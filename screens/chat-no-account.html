I started taking Brufen 3 days ago. Can I take it on an empty stomach?
It's best to take Brufen (ibuprofen) with food or milk to protect your stomach lining. Taking it on an empty stomach can increase the risk of irritation or discomfort. If you experience stomach pain, nausea, or heartburn, take it with a meal or a full glass of milk.
