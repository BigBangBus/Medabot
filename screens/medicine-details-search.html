Jardiance

25mg | Pill
Source: Infarmed | Check full Leafleat
Interactions
Use caution when combined with insulin or other medicines that lower blood sugar.
This pill nullifies the effect of birth controls such as Clarissa.
This pill with Ben-U-Ron can give you diarrhea.

What is used for:

Type 2 Diabetes: As an adjunct to diet and exercise to improve glycemic control.

Heart Failure: To reduce the risk of cardiovascular death and hospitalization.

Kidney Disease: To reduce the risk of further decline, end-stage kidney disease, and death.

Key details

Dosage: Typically taken orally once daily in the morning, with or without food. Initial dose is often 10mg, which may be increased to 25mg.

Usage information

Administration:
Taken in the morning, with or without food.
Adjustment:
Dosage may be increased by a healthcare provider for additional glycemic control in type 2 diabetes, provided the initial dose is tolerated.
Maximum Level:
Once daily in the morning.

Warnings and Precautions:

Infections: It may cause serious urinary tract and genital infections.

Hypoglycemia: Risk increases when taken with insulin or sulfonylureas.

Kidney Function: It should not be initiated in patients with severe kidney problems.
